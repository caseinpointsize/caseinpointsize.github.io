Excel in Learning. Excel in Life.

Welcome to The Goodwill Excel Center. It’s the first free public charter high school in Texas that provides adults ages 17-50 the opportunity to earn their high school diploma, complete an in-demand professional certification, and begin post-secondary education.

The Diploma Difference

What a difference a diploma makes. By completing high school and receiving your diploma, did you know that you will incrase your earning power by up to $400,000 over your lifetime? Education really does make an impact on getting the job, salary, and career that you want.

$1600

High school diploma holders earned approximately $1600 more in mean monthly earnings compared with GE certificate holders.
*2009 US Census Bureau

50%

Children of parents that have earned their high school diploma are 50% more likely to complete their high school education.
