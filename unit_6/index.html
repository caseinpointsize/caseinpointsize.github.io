Excel in Learning. Excel in Life.

Welcome to The Goodwill Excel Center. It’s the first free public charter high school in Texas that provides adults ages 17-50 the opportunity to earn their high school diploma, complete an in-demand professional certification, and begin post-secondary education. Obtaining your high school diploma can make the difference between getting a job and getting the job that you want. And with the additional education and training available, you will be able to enter the job market more quickly, command a higher salary, and turn a job into a long-term, sustainable career.

...Goodwill opens their arms and helps you…there are no closed doors, and they never let you fail.

--Kaitlen, Graduate

The Diploma Difference

What a difference a diploma makes. By completing high school and receiving your diploma, did you know that you will incrase your earning power by up to $400,000 over your lifetime? Education really does make an impact on getting the job, salary, and career that you want.

$1600

High school diploma holders earned approximately $1600 more in mean monthly earnings compared with GE certificate holders.

*2009 US Census Bureau

50%

Children of parents that have earned their high school diploma are 50% more likely to complete their high school education.
