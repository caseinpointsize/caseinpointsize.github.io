About

Our Mission

Goodwill Central Texas has a vision – empowering 100,000 Central Texans to transform their lives through work. Education is paramount to achieving that vision – a high school education can open doors and create opportunities for a sustainable career. And that’s our shared mission…generating lifelong connections to work.

The Goodwill Excel Center Difference

Specialized learning plans, life coaches, and other services are just some of the ways we empower students to succeed. Graduates may also start working with an in-demand career certification through the Goodwill Career and Technical Academy, and can pursue post-secondary education.

    FREE childcare
  • Transportation vouchers
  • An individualized and flexible year-round curriculum – Night Classes now available!
  • Life Coaches and support services
  • Eight-week sessions in small classes
  • ESL and accessible services available
  • Tuition assistance for industry-recognized certificates or dual enrollment classes at Austin Community College

Ready to Take the First Step?

If you're reading this, you've already taken the first step to changing your life and the life of your family. You want to complete your high school education - The Goodwill Excel Center helps you make it possible.
